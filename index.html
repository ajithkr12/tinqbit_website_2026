FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often from clients exploring our services.

What services does Tinqbit provide?

Tinqbit is a full-service digital marketing and IT solutions agency. We offer web development, mobile app development, custom software development, AI & automation, branding, and digital marketing services such as SEO, social media, meta advertising, email marketing, and content marketing — everything a business needs to build and grow its digital presence under one roof.

What technologies does Tinqbit use?

We work with a modern, flexible tech stack chosen to fit each project's needs — including React, Angular, Vue, Node.js, PHP/Laravel, and Python for web and software development, Flutter and React Native for mobile, and cloud platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure. For AI-driven projects, we leverage leading AI models and automation tools to deliver reliable, scalable solutions.

Do you build mobile applications for Android and iOS?

Yes. Our mobile development team builds native Android and iOS apps, as well as cross-platform apps using Flutter and React Native — so you can launch on both platforms from a single codebase if that suits your budget and timeline best.

Can Tinqbit develop AI-powered solutions?

Absolutely. We design and build AI-powered solutions including chatbots, intelligent process automation, predictive analytics, recommendation engines, and integrations with large language models — helping you automate repetitive tasks, improve customer experience, and unlock insights from your data.

How much does software development cost?

Costs vary depending on the scope, complexity, features, and timeline of your project. After an initial consultation to understand your requirements, we provide a transparent, itemized quote with flexible engagement models — fixed price, milestone-based, or dedicated team — so there are no hidden costs.

What digital marketing services do you offer?

Our digital marketing services include search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, meta advertising, Google Ads/PPC, email marketing, content marketing, and branding — all backed by data-driven strategy and regular performance reporting.

How long does it take to develop a software solution?

Timelines depend on the project's scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a custom software platform or mobile app can take 2-4 months. During our initial discovery call, we'll give you a realistic project roadmap and milestones tailored to your requirements.

Do you provide website development services?

Yes, website development is one of our core services. We design and build responsive, SEO-friendly websites — from business and portfolio sites to e-commerce stores and custom web applications — using modern frameworks and best practices for performance and security.

Does Tinqbit serve international clients?

Yes. With offices in India, Dubai, and Germany, we work with clients across the globe. Our remote-friendly workflows, dedicated account managers, and flexible scheduling make it easy to collaborate no matter where you're located.
